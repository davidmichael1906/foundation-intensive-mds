The Intellectual Foundation
The Book Behind the Process
The Unconscious Actor;
Working Out of Control in Full Command
by Darryl Hickman

Developed by Hollywood actor and master teacher Darryl Hickman through a lifelong quest rooted in Stanislavsky principles, this book is the definitive written expression of the process you will live inside during the intensive. It represents the most original contribution to the literature of acting craft in a generation — a complete, learnable system that takes the actor from technique to truth.

The Foundation Intensive is built from this foundation, taught by one of its foremost champions. Many participants find that reading the book after the week deepens and crystallizes everything they experienced in the room.

The Method

Out of Control.
In Full Command.

A rigorous physical, emotional, and analytical journey built from Darryl Hickman's lifetime of research. Not a philosophy to admire — a discipline to inhabit.

01
The Physical Foundation

Name & Bow, The Numbers, and the Characterization Exercise — daily work that frees the instrument and enforces the core principle: Acting is Physical.

02
Scene Immersion

Scenes from celebrated playwrights, assigned in advance. You arrive off-book, having rehearsed — ready to work from day one, not learn lines.

03
Daily Personal Coaching

Michael works with every scene, every actor, every day. No passive observation — you are in it, or learning from watching others in theirs.

04
The Unconscious State

The destination: full command in which technique becomes invisible and only truth remains. Not mystical — systematically built, day by day.

05
Outside Work

Partners continue working between sessions throughout the week. The process lives in you between the rooms — not just on the floor.

06
The Showcase

The week closes with a final showcase for industry, family, and friends — the most developed scenes performed for real stakes, for a real audience.
